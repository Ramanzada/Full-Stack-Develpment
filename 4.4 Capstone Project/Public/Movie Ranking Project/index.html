The BEst Movies According to Raman

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A banker is wrongly imprisoned and forms a powerful friendship while secretly planning his escape. A story of hope, patience, and freedom.

Inception (2010)

A skilled thief enters people’s dreams to steal secrets, but he’s hired for the impossible: planting an idea in someone’s mind. A mix of action, psychology, and mind-bending visuals.

Parasite (2019)

A poor family infiltrates the lives of a wealthy household, leading to darkly funny and shocking consequences. A sharp critique of social inequality.
